SERVICES

The MVP (Minimum Viable Product) stage of the product life cycle is handled by USS, along with product support and maintenance. Being a trailblazer and frontrunner in Sri Lanka's IT sector, we have a solid reputation and draw in the top local talent.

Biz POS SYSTEM

Biz specializing in POS systems focuses its efforts on developing tailored solutions for various industries, such as retail, hospitality, or healthcare. Understanding the unique needs and challenges of each sector, the company designs and implements POS software equipped with features like sales processing, inventory management, and robust reporting capabilities.

BIZ Barcode QC

BizBarcode, a company specializing in barcode quality control (QC) systems, is dedicated to providing tailored solutions for industries reliant on efficient barcode management, such as manufacturing, logistics, and retail. Leveraging advanced technologies, BizBarcode develops robust QC software capable of verifying barcode accuracy, readability, and compliance with industry standards.

BIZ Meal Token System

BIZ Meal Token System specializes in providing comprehensive solutions for managing employees' meal reservations and assisting HR departments in tracking individual meal quantities efficiently. Our innovative software streamlines the process of meal reservation, allowing employees to easily book their meals while providing HR personnel with the tools to monitor and trace each employee's meal quantity.

Biz CRM System

BIZ CRM System specializes in providing comprehensive customer relationship management solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of businesses across various industries. Leveraging advanced technology, our CRM software offers robust features for managing customer interactions, tracking leads, and analyzing data to drive informed decision-making.
