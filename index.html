Services

The MVP (Minimum Viable Product) stage of the product life cycle is handled by USS, along with product support and maintenance. Being a trailblazer and frontrunner in Sri Lanka's IT sector, we have a solid reputation and draw in the top local talent.

INVENTRY & SALES MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

We developed "Unique IMS" to help businesses manage their inventory and sales force more strategically and to eliminate the headaches associated with inventory management and sales (IMS).

ERP SYSTEM

In USS, the ERP system serves as a comprehensive solution integrating various business processes like finance, inventory, HR, and supply chain management, streamlining operations, enhancing efficiency, and facilitating informed decision-making.

Innovative Systems

USS pioneers innovative systems, leveraging cutting-edge technologies and creative solutions to address complex challenges and drive meaningful advancements in software development and implementation.

FINANCIAL SERVICES MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

A microfinance programme called "UNIQUE FINSERV" makes it possible to manage all financial calculations and other services with only one programme.

CONSTRUCTION MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

One programme that has transformed the building sector is called "UNIQUE CONEX." It brings all of the recorded approvals and check lists into the SMART era through automation. For the purpose of making decisions, all of the data used in the building process is moved to the big data platform.

RECOVERY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

The Recovery Management System implemented by USS Company ensures robust processes for data backup, disaster recovery, and continuity planning, safeguarding critical information, minimizing downtime, and enabling swift restoration of operations in the event of disruptions.
